Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how My Bible handles information. The app is published by zhulonghai. For privacy questions, contact wuyulaopo@gmail.com.

Information Stored on Your Device

My Bible is designed as an offline-first Bible reading and daily devotional app. The app stores the following information locally on your device to provide app features:

  • Reading position and reader appearance preferences.
  • Highlights, bookmarks, notes, favorites, and Amen actions.
  • Reminder preferences for morning and evening notifications.
  • Downloaded Bible audio files and audio download task records.
  • App settings such as language, theme, and notification choices.

This local app data is not sent to the developer's server. My Bible does not require an account and does not provide cloud sync in this version.

Advertising and Third-Party SDKs

My Bible uses Google Mobile Ads SDK / AdMob to show ads, including app open, native, and banner ads. According to Google's documentation, the Google Mobile Ads SDK may automatically collect and share data such as IP address, user product interactions, diagnostic information, and device or account identifiers for advertising, analytics, and fraud prevention. Data handled by Google is subject to Google's policies, including How Google uses information from sites or apps that use Google services.

The app does not ask you to create an account for advertising. Advertising identifiers can be reset or deleted through your Android device settings where supported.

Bible Audio and Network Requests

My Bible can stream or download chapter MP3 audio from third-party Bible content sources such as eBible.org. When your device requests audio files, the audio provider may receive standard network information such as your IP address, device/browser network headers, and request timing. Downloaded audio files are stored in the app's private storage area on your device and can be deleted from the app's Audio Downloads page or by clearing app data.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, My Bible uses local notifications for morning and evening reminders. Reminder preferences are stored on your device. The app does not use reminders to collect or transmit your personal content.

Sharing

If you choose to share a verse or devotional content, the share action is initiated by you and handled by the app or service you select from your device's share sheet. The selected third-party app's privacy practices apply to that sharing action.

Data Retention and Deletion

Local app data remains on your device until you delete it in the app, delete downloaded audio, clear app data, or uninstall the app. Because My Bible does not provide user accounts in this version, there is no separate account deletion process.

Security

My Bible stores app data in local device storage. Network requests for remote audio and ads use the transport security provided by the relevant service providers. No method of storage or transmission is perfect, but the app is designed to minimize developer-side collection by keeping reading data local.

Children

My Bible is not specifically directed to children under the age that requires parental consent in their jurisdiction. If you configure Google Play target audience settings to include children, you must ensure the app, ads, and SDK configuration comply with Google Play Families policies and applicable child privacy laws.

Changes

This policy may be updated when app features, SDKs, or legal requirements change. The effective date above will be updated when material changes are made.
