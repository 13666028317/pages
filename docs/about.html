About My Bible

Last updated: April 30, 2026

My Bible is an offline-first Bible reading and daily devotional app. It includes Bible reading, search, bookmarks, highlights, notes, favorites, Amen actions, daily devotion content, reader appearance settings, local reminders, and chapter-level Bible audio.

Content Sources

  • WEBBE Bible text and chapter MP3 audio are sourced from eBible.org and the World English Bible British Edition public domain release.
  • Other Scripture translations, daily verses, and devotional content are bundled locally from existing project assets or other lawful sources.
  • Remote Bible audio may require internet access for streaming or downloading.
